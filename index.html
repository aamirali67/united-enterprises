Field Note

What is a high‑pressure fitting?

High pressure fittings are specialized components engineered to securely connect pipes, tubes, or hoses in demanding systems operating from 3,000 to 150,000 PSI. They are essential for preventing leaks and ensuring safety in hydraulic machinery, water blasters, oil and gas applications, and hydrostatic testing.

Why SS316 is the only grade worth stocking.

  • Corrosion resistance

    Molybdenum content gives SS316 real resistance against chlorides and industrial moisture — where standard steel would pit and fail.

  • Rated for sustained pressure

    Every union, elbow, tee and valve in the line is built to hold its seal under continuous high-pressure CNG and industrial flow.

  • Precision-machined tolerances

    Tight ferrule and thread tolerances mean fewer leaks, fewer callbacks, and connections that seat right the first time.

  • Built for the long run

    Fittings that outlast the systems they're installed in — less downtime, less re-fitting, lower total cost.
